User Study - virtual object insertion

You will be shown pairs of images where a virtual object has been inserted. You are asked to answer which image looks more realistic, by considering both the appearance of the object and it's effect on the background.

In order to correctly see the images, it is recommended to answer this form on a computer (not a phone) and to zoom in to see in fullscreen the two images
See the example below


Please take a total of only 5 seconds to examine both images shown. If both images look equally good, select one randomly. A total of 160 pairs will be shown, which should take about 10 minutes. Your answer is automatically saved when you go to the next image. Thank you for your participation!
